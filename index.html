Why MajorMatch?

Every year, millions of students worldwide face mental health and financial stress because they end up in college majors they were pushed into by peers, family, or short-term pressures. These mismatches can cause years of lost time, extra debt, and reduced well-being. MajorMatch is a non-profit project that uses data - including academic strengths, interests and the Big Five personality profile - to provide compassionate, data-backed suggestions that help students make informed choices about their undergraduate stream.

Millions+ Students globally affected by misaligned majors

How data & Big Five help

Personality measures such as the Big Five (Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism) - when combined with students' expressed interests, hobbies, academic strengths and future goals - give a fuller picture of which undergraduate streams will likely fit a student's motivations and learning style. We treat these signals as contributing suggestions - not mandates - and present options with pros, cons and confidence scores.

How the AI & ML behind MajorMatch works

Behind the scenes, MajorMatch uses a combination of data preprocessing, personality trait mapping, and advanced machine learning models to generate tailored undergraduate stream recommendations.

1
Data preprocessing
We collect inputs such as Big Five personality scores, aptitudes (numerical, spatial, verbal, etc.), and goals. Data is standardized to ensure fair comparisons across different traits and skills.
2
Random Forest Classifier
A balanced Random Forest model is trained on past career and aptitude data to predict suitable majors. It evaluates patterns across hundreds of decision trees to minimize bias and improve reliability.
3
Confidence Scoring
Instead of giving one fixed answer, the model outputs probabilities for multiple majors. We present the top three matches with confidence percentages, helping students see options.
4
Personality Insight
Big Five traits (like Openness or Conscientiousness) are analyzed to highlight the studentâ€™s dominant strength and explain how it connects to different career paths.
Our approach ensures that recommendations are not mandates - they are evidence-backed suggestions combining personality, aptitude, and data-driven insights, always leaving the final decision to the student.
